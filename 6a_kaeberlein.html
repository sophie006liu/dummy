How much biology have I done?

I have also interned at the University of Washington Kaeberlein lab where I studied C. elegans and how we can delay the onset of Alzheimer's disease which was indicated by paralysis.

My work was focused on validating the performance of the WormBot, which is a machine that conducts 144 parallel C. elegans phenotyping experiments.

I am very grateful to be listed as a co-author in the final WormBot paper !
