A Little About My Professional Interests

I love protein structures, analyzing gene transcripts, synthetic biology... I love all things biology!

As much as I enjoy a good qt-PCR session with the team, I can always appreciate a good coding challenge in Python, SML, Java, C, C#, C+, C-, C^2, sqrt(C), ln(C)...

At CMU, I can live the best of both worlds by solving biological problems using computational methods.
